## Payment Service Metrics
- Capture execution rates for standard customer billing events.
- Audit refund and compensation workflow outcomes.

### Payment Processing
- `payment.requests.total`: Total payment requests received
- `payment.completed.total`: Successfully completed payments
- `payment.failed.total`: Failed payment executions

### Refunds and Financial Adjustments
- `payment.refund.requests.total`: Total refund requests received
- `payment.refund.completed.total`: Successfully completed refunds
- `payment.refund.failed.total`: Failed refund executions
